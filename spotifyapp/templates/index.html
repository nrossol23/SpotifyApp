Welcome to SpotifyApp!

Click the link at the top left of the page to connect your Spotify account and gain access to detailed statistics and recommendations related to your listening activity. This app's back-end was created using Python and Flask. The front end was created using Bootstrap 4.0 and a small amount of JavaScript. This app does not use any database. Instead, the extremely small amount of user-specific data needed is stored inside a cookie.

The purpose of this project was to gain a deeper understanding of utilizing third-party APIs. In this app's case, we make use of Spotify's public Web API.
